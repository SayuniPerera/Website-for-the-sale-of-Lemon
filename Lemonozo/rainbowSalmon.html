Rainbow Salmon Skewers

Ingredients

  • 6 wooden skewers
  • 1-1/2 lbs. skinless, boneless salmon fillet, cut into 2" pieces
  • 1/2 each red, green, yellow, and orange peppers, cut into 2" pieces
  • 1 large red onion, cut into 2" pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, melted
  • Zest from 1 Lemonozo Lemon
  • Juice from 1 Lemonozo Lemon
  • Lemonozo Lemon wedges, for serving

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil; set aside. Thread your skewers with pieces of salmon, pepper, and onion. Repeat with remaining ingredients until the skewers are complete. Brush each complete skewer with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Bake for about 10 minutes until cooked through, turning once. In a small bowl, combine the butter, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Drizzle the lemon butter over the skewers just before serving. Serve with lemon wedges.
