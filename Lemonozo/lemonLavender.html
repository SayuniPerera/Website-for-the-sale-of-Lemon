Lemon Lavender Spritz

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup ricotta, divided
  • Zest from 1 Lemonozo Lemon, plus more for garnish
  • Juice from 1 Lemonozo Lemon
  • Maple syrup, for serving (optional)

Preparation

For the lavender syrup: Combine 1 cup water, 1/2 cup granulated sugar and 2 tablespoons dried culinary lavender in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low and simmer for about 15 minutes until the mixture has thickened. Allow to rest for 15 minutes. Place syrup in the refrigerator to cool down completely before using.

For the spritz: In a small shaker, combine the vodka, lemon juice, and lavender syrup with ice. Shake vigorously. Strain into a glass filled with ice. Top with club soda and garnish with a slice of lemon and culinary lavender sprig.
